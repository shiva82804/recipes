Mutton Biryani

Ingredients

  • Mutton
  • Rice
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Green Chillies
  • Coriander Leaves
  • Mint Leaves
  • Yogurt
  • Ginger Garlic Paste
  • Red Chilli Powder
  • Turmeric Powder
  • Garam Masala
  • Coriander Powder
  • Cumin Powder
  • Bay Leaves
  • Cloves
  • Cinnamon
  • Cardamom
  • Star Anise
  • Black Stone Flower
  • Shahi Jeera
  • Salt
  • Ghee
  • Oil
  • Water

Instructions

Steps required for making Mutton Biryani are as follows:

  1. Wash and soak the rice for 30 minutes.
  2. Heat oil and ghee in a pressure cooker.
  3. Add the whole spices and saute for a minute.
  4. Add the sliced onions and saute until they turn golden brown.
  5. Add the ginger garlic paste and saute until the raw smell goes off.
  6. Add the chopped tomatoes and cook until they turn mushy.
  7. Add the mutton pieces and cook until they turn brown.
  8. Add the yogurt and all the spice powders and mix well.
  9. Add the chopped coriander and mint leaves and mix well.
  10. Add water and pressure cook for 4 whistles.
  11. Once the pressure releases, add the soaked rice and mix well.
  12. Pressure cook for 2 whistles.
  13. Once the pressure releases, fluff the rice and serve hot.
